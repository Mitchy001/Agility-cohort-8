Pilau Recipe

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef or chicken (cubed)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cups broth
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Fry onions in oil until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, and pilau masala.
  3. Add meat and cook until browned.
  4. Mix in tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Pour in broth, season with salt, and bring to a boil.
  6. Add washed rice, cover, and simmer until cooked.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Beef Stew Recipe

    Ingredients

  • 500g beef (cubed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 2 tomatoes (blended or chopped)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until soft.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and spices, stirring well.
  3. Add beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and tomato paste, cook until thick.
  5. Add water/broth and simmer until beef is tender.
  6. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.
